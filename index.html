Company Profile

Built on Integrity.
Trading Across Borders.

AES Collective Pte. Ltd. is a Singapore-registered international commodity trading company operating under ACRA-verified status. We specialize in Energy, Metals, and Agricultural commodities, connecting suppliers and buyers across Asia-Pacific, Europe, and Africa.

Our company is structured as an Exempt Private Company Limited by Shares, with SGD 100,000 in fully paid-up capital. Sole shareholder and Director is Lim Chin Poh, a Singapore Citizen with full signatory authority over all commercial and contractual matters.

We operate strictly within international petroleum trading norms, sourcing only from non-sanctioned origins with third-party verification at every delivery point through SGS, Bureau Veritas (BV), and Intertek.
