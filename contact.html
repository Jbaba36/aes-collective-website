Before You Reach Out

What to include in your first message.

Help us respond with substance. A complete inquiry lets us return a working quotation within 24–72 hours.

01

Product & specification

Product (Diesel EN590, LNG, LPG, crude), required grade or quality, quantity in MT or barrels.

02

Delivery profile

Discharge port, target laycan, single shipment or schedule, preferred Incoterm (CIF / FOB).

03

Counterparty & instrument

Buyer corporate name, country of incorporation, payment instrument (DLC / SBLC) and issuing bank.

A signed Letter of Intent (LOI) or Irrevocable Corporate Purchase Order (ICPO) accelerates the response materially.
